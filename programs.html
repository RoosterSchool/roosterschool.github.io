Comprehensive Learning Programs

At Rooster Academy, we offer a complete educational experience designed to develop your child's academic foundation and cognitive abilities. Our programs are tailored for different age groups and learning levels.

Our Core Programs

SIP Abacus Program

Our flagship program in partnership with SIP Academy India

The SIP Abacus program is designed to enhance mental arithmetic skills, concentration, and overall cognitive development. Students learn to perform complex calculations mentally, improving their mathematical confidence and speed.

Key Benefits:
  • Mental arithmetic mastery
  • Enhanced concentration and focus
  • Improved memory and recall
  • Boosted mathematical confidence
  • Better problem-solving abilities

Centum Math Program

Comprehensive mathematical skill development

Our Centum Math program follows an individualized approach, allowing students to progress at their own pace. Starting from basic arithmetic to advanced concepts, we build a strong foundation for mathematical excellence.

Program Features:
  • Individualized learning pace
  • Step-by-step progression
  • Advanced topics beyond grade level
  • Strong foundation in arithmetic

Junior Oxford English Program

Comprehensive English language development

Our Junior Oxford English program focuses on developing strong reading, writing, and communication skills. Through carefully designed worksheets and activities, students build vocabulary, grammar, and comprehension abilities.

Program Highlights:
  • Reading comprehension skills
  • Vocabulary and grammar development
  • Writing and communication skills

Rooster Robotics

Coding & Robotics for Kids

Our Rooster Robotics program introduces children to the exciting world of coding and robotics through hands-on learning experiences. Students learn programming concepts while building and controlling robots.

Program Features:
  • Block-based programming (Scratch)
  • Python programming fundamentals
  • Robot building and assembly
  • Problem-solving and logical thinking
  • STEM skills development

Rainbow Art

Drawing & Art Program for Kids

Rainbow Art nurtures creativity and artistic expression in children through various drawing and painting techniques. Students develop fine motor skills while exploring different art forms and mediums.

Art Skills Covered:
  • Basic drawing techniques
  • Color theory and mixing
  • Painting with different mediums
  • Creative expression and imagination
  • Art appreciation and history
