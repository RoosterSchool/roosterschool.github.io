Our Mission

At Rooster Academy, our mission is to unlock every child's potential through systematic skill development programs. We believe that every child is inherently smart and capable of achieving remarkable cognitive growth when provided with the right tools and guidance.

Through our partnership with SIP Abacus, we aim to develop not just mathematical skills, but also enhance concentration, memory, logical thinking, and overall brain development in children aged 6-12 years.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in our community has access to world-class skill development programs that prepare them for academic excellence and life-long success.

Our vision extends beyond traditional education to create confident, creative, and capable individuals who can think critically and solve problems effectively in an ever-changing world.

Our Story

Rooster Academy was established with a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves the opportunity to develop their full cognitive potential. Recognizing the transformative impact of systematic skill development, we partnered with SIP Academy India to bring world-class abacus training to our local communities.

Starting with a single center, we have grown to serve multiple locations across Pallipalayam, Sankari, and Komarapalayam. Our journey has been marked by the success stories of thousands of children who have discovered their innate abilities and developed confidence in their mathematical and cognitive skills.

Today, we are proud to be an approved center of SIP Abacus, serving as a trusted partner in children's educational development across Tamil Nadu.
